This document contains information about the following credit cards:
- HDFC Infinia metal card
- ICICI Sapphiro Credit Card
- SBI Card Prime

---

Question: I am travelling from New Delhi to Thailand. What card should I use for booking tickets and hotels for maximum benefits?
Answer:
- **HDFC Infinia:** The best option. Use it via the SmartBuy portal to earn 5x reward points (~5% back) and get unlimited domestic & international lounge access.
- **ICICI Sapphiro:** Lower returns (4 pts/₹100 ≈ 1%). Keep as a backup.
- **SBI Prime:** Lower returns (2 pts/₹100). Keep as a backup.

---

Question: I have to get petrol; which of my cards is best for fuel?
Answer:
- **ICICI Sapphiro:** The best option. It auto-credits the 1% fuel surcharge waiver on spends up to ₹4,000.
- **HDFC Infinia:** Waives the 1% surcharge but earns no fuel points.
- **SBI Prime:** Waives the 1% surcharge but earns no fuel points.

---

Question: Which of my cards should I use for movie ticket booking?
Answer:
- **ICICI Sapphiro:** The best option. Offers Buy-1-Get-1 on BookMyShow twice a month (up to a ₹500 cap).
- **SBI Prime:** Good backup. Offers one free ticket per month (up to a ₹250 cap) after the ICICI quota is used.
- **HDFC Infinia:** Has no standing movie offer.

---

Question: Which of my cards has the maximum airport lounge benefits? Please provide numbers for domestic and international access.
Answer:
- **HDFC Infinia:** Unlimited domestic and international lounge visits via Priority Pass for primary and add-on cardholders.
- **ICICI Sapphiro:** 4 domestic visits per quarter (requires ₹75k spend in the previous quarter) and 2 international visits per year.
- **SBI Prime:** 8 domestic visits per year (max 2 per quarter) and 4 international visits per year via Priority Pass (max 2 per quarter).

---

Question: What is the reward rate on my HDFC card?
Answer:
- **Base Reward Rate:** 5 Reward Points per ₹150 spent (~3.3% back).
- **Accelerated Rewards:** Up to 10x points on SmartBuy partners.
- **Point Value:**
    - ₹1 for flights and hotels on SmartBuy.
    - ~₹0.50 for gift cards.

---

Question: Which card has the lowest forex markup for international spending?
Answer:
- **HDFC Infinia:** 2% (This is the lowest).
- **ICICI Sapphiro:** 3.5%.
- **SBI Prime:** 3.5%.

---

Question: Does my card offer Club Marriott or similar memberships?
Answer:
- **HDFC Infinia:** Yes, a complimentary Club Marriott membership for the first year.
- **SBI Prime:** Yes, a Trident Privilege Red membership is available on request.
- **ICICI Sapphiro:** No, it has no hotel-loyalty tie-ins.

---

Question: What are the hidden perks of my cards?
Answer:
- **HDFC Infinia:** Unlimited worldwide golf rounds & lessons, and a 24x7 concierge service.
- **ICICI Sapphiro:** Up to 4 complimentary golf rounds per month (on meeting a spend trigger) and 2 free international lounge visits per year via Priority Pass.
- **SBI Prime:** Milestone e-vouchers, including a ₹1000 Pizza Hut voucher for spending ₹50k per quarter, and a ₹7,000 Yatra/Pantaloons voucher for spending ₹5L annually.
